Career Goals

Short-Term Goals

My short-term goal is to gain hands-on experience in UI/UX design by working on diverse projects and collaborating with experienced professionals in the field. I aim to refine my skills and deepen my understanding of user-centered design principles.

Long-Term Goals

In the long term, I aspire to take on leadership roles in UI/UX design, where I can mentor junior designers, lead design teams, and contribute to the strategic direction of product development. I also aim to make a meaningful impact by advocating for inclusive and accessible design practices.
